Boiled Eggs

Boiled eggs are a classic, and they're super easy to make. Read about how in this short article.


Ingredients/Materials:

  • Some eggs (make however many your heart desires)
  • Water (your sink will do)
  • A pot for boiling
  • An extra bowl
  • A mesh net or ladle (to scoop the egg out with)

Instructions:

  1. Put your egg in the pot and add about an inch or two of water.
  2. Turn your stove to high heat. Leave your pot on the stove until it the water starts boiling (steam will rise out of the pot).
  3. After you see that steam rise up, wait for 5 - 10 minutes. For a soft boiled egg, wait for 5-6 minutes, for medium boiled, wait for 7 - 8 minutes, and for hard boiled, wait for 9 - 10 minutes.
  4. Once the time is up, scoop your egg out of the pot, place it into the bowl, and run it with cold water for about 15 - 30 seconds.
  5. Your egg is now ready to eat! Peel and enjoy :)
